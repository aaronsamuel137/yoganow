About

Yoga NOW Boulder aims to display all the yoga classes happening in Boulder within the next few hours. This spares one the trouble of having to search through many studio sites just to find an interesting class. As of now, only Yoga Pod and Yoga Workshop are supported, but there are plans to support more and more as time goes on.


Want your studio to be included on Yoga NOW Boulder? Email aaron@yoganowboulder.com.
